About Us

Steve Jobs once said, If you want to make everyone happy, don’t become a leader, sell ice cream. We took that statement too much to heart. We have always wanted to make the people around us happy, and what better way to do that than to sell ice cream?, with all the goodies of desserts?

Doing that, we wanted to become your favorite, so we decided to call ourselves “favorie”.

Taking baby steps, we’ve moved from a stand, to a kiosk and now, to a shop, with a lot underway.

Thank you for making us your favorite.
